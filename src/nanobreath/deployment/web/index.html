BreathCoach detects audible breaths frame-by-frame using a small (~15K parameter) causal neural network on top of a frozen NanoPitch pitch-tracking backbone. Trained on VocalSet with weak labels from the Ruinskiy & Lavner 2007 algorithm (the Respiro-en self-training recipe adapted to singing). The pink markers above are that 2007 DSP baseline running on the same audio, for comparison.

Built on NanoPitch. Real-time, mic-only, runs in your browser.
